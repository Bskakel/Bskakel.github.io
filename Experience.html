Robotics

I am the current president and co-founder of Excelsiors robotics team. As President I call together meetings. I help decide where our funding goes. I also supervise meetings and work with the vise president to create groups within our club to carry out projects. We compete in competitions held by FTC. Each year they create a new task or challenge for competitors robots to complete.

Public Speaking

I have had many opportunities to practice my public speaking skills. From fourth to sixth grade I helped run assemblies every friday morning in front of the entire school. In high school I helped with open house where I spoke about how Excelsior was a great school. I also went to the middle schools in my district speaking about Excelsior and answering questions from 8th graders.

Junior Honor Society

In junior honor society I helped run fundraisers and blood drives. We had 2 blood drives each year where we invited people to donate blood for the Red Cross. I also attended a leadership program where I went to D.C for a week during the summer and learned about history and current events.
